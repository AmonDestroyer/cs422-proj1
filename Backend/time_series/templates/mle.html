Time Series Problems

Picking a problem from the Problems page and uploading it to the MLE page helps to calculate the error mean of the model. This step is crucial for model evaluation, as it identifies the accuracy of the predictions and areas that require improvement. Regular testing with different datasets allows for identifying and fixing errors, ensuring the model remains effective.

Choose which problem you would like to solve from the problems page. Hit next when you have your solution with the set id attached.

Uploading your solution

To ensure the accuracy of your data analysis results, it's essential to check them against the original data. After developing a solution, you can test it against a separate set of data or compare the results manually with the original data. By doing so, you can identify errors and inconsistencies and refine your approach to data analysis. To check your solution, submit your solution by uploading a txt, csv, JSON, exel, or xlsx file. You will be able to see how your solution compares.
